# End-to-End Customer Retention & Behavioral Analytics Pipeline

## 📌 Project Business Overview & Strategic Value
In the retail industry, customer acquisition costs are significantly higher than retention costs. To maximize profitability, a business must move away from generic marketing strategies and leverage granular behavioral data to drive customer engagement.

**The Objective:** This project builds a comprehensive, multi-layered data analytics pipeline to analyze customer shopping trends, track brand loyalty, and identify churn risks. Using a raw dataset of over one million transactional records, I designed a pipeline that handles data ingestion, executes advanced behavioral logic via a relational database, and surfaces key insights through an interactive executive dashboard.

### 📊 Core Business Problems Addressed:
1. **RFM Customer Segmentation:** Categorizing customers dynamically based on how recently they purchased (**Recency**), how often they buy (**Frequency**), and how much they spend (**Monetary Value**).
2. **Cohort Retention Analysis:** Tracking monthly customer "birth cohorts" to measure how customer retention percentages degrade over a 12-month lifecycle.
3. **Product Velocity Drivers:** Identifying the top-selling product lines by volume to help optimize inventory and supply chain decisions.
