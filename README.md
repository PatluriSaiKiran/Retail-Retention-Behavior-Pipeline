# End-to-End Customer Retention & Behavioral Analytics Pipeline

## 📌 Project Business Overview & Strategic Value
In the retail industry, customer acquisition costs are significantly higher than retention costs. To maximize profitability, a business must move away from generic marketing strategies and leverage granular behavioral data to drive customer engagement.

**The Objective:** This project builds a comprehensive, multi-layered data analytics pipeline to analyze customer shopping trends, track brand loyalty, and identify churn risks. Using a raw dataset of over one million transactional records, I designed a pipeline that handles data ingestion, executes advanced behavioral logic via a relational database, and surfaces key insights through an interactive executive dashboard.

### 📊 Core Business Problems Addressed:
1. **RFM Customer Segmentation:** Categorizing customers dynamically based on how recently they purchased (**Recency**), how often they buy (**Frequency**), and how much they spend (**Monetary Value**).
2. **Cohort Retention Analysis:** Tracking monthly customer "birth cohorts" to measure how customer retention percentages degrade over a 12-month lifecycle.
3. **Product Velocity Drivers:** Identifying the top-selling product lines by volume to help optimize inventory and supply chain decisions.

---

## 🏗️ Data Architecture & Flow
1. **Ingestion (SQL):** Imported raw transaction logs (`online_retail_II.csv`) and explicitly cast variables (dates, quantities, prices) to ensure structural integrity.
2. **Analytics (SQL):** Ran a master query script (`Customer_Retention_Analysis.sql`) to clean anomalies, run mathematical metrics, and build cohort matrices.
3. **Storage (CSV):** Exported clean analytical tables (`RFM.data.csv`, `cohort_data.csv`) to serve as light, reliable data sources for reporting.
4. **Visualization (Power BI):** Imported processed datasets into Power BI (`cc.pbix`) using an optimized Star Schema model for real-time dashboard interaction.

---

## 📂 Repository Structure
* **`Data/online_retail_II.csv`**: Raw, historical transactional data.
* **`Processed_Data/RFM.data.csv`**: Pre-calculated customer segments generated by SQL queries.
* **`Processed_Data/cohort_data.csv`**: Pre-calculated monthly customer retention percentages.
* **`SQL_Scripts/Customer_Retention_Analysis.sql`**: Full database script containing data cleaning, CTEs, and segmentation logic.
* **`Dashboards/cc.pbix`**: Interactive Power BI executive dashboard file.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
* **SQL Engine:** Advanced Window Functions (`NTILE()`, `OVER()`), Common Table Expressions (CTEs), Type Casting, Data Aggregations.
* **Power BI:** Star Schema Data Modeling, Calculated Measures, DAX Formulas (`CALCULATE`, `DIVIDE`), Interactive UI Layout Design.
* **Business Frameworks:** RFM Analysis, Cohort Retention Matrices, Customer Life Cycle Tracking.

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights & Actions

* **Nurture High-Value "Champions":** Isolated premium consumers with top-tier purchase recency and frequency scores.
  * *Action:* Launch a tier-based VIP loyalty program to maximize wallet share.
* **Win Back "Sleeping Giants":** Flagged high-spending, historically frequent buyers who have not returned recently.
  * *Action:* Deploy an automated email campaign featuring tailored discounts on their past favorite categories to reduce churn.
* **Fix Month-2 Drop-offs:** Identified a sharp, repeating drop in active buyers exactly 30 days after their first transaction.
  * *Action:* Introduce an automated post-purchase incentive trigger 25 days after onboarding to encourage a second visit.
